Match Analysis

You can't improve what you don't measure. A simple charting habit turns "I played badly" into "my second-serve points won dropped to 38%." Print a sheet, tally a match, find the truth.

How to chart (simple tally method)

You don't need an app. A friend (or you, from video) tallies a few key events per point. Start with just three stats, add more as it becomes habit:

  • Serve outcome (1st in / 2nd in / double fault)
  • How the point ended (winner / forced error / unforced error — and by whom)
  • Rally length (1–4 / 5–8 / 9+)

⚠️ Common mistakes
  • Charting 20 stats and quitting after one match — start with 3, stay consistent.
  • Judging a match on feel/result instead of the numbers.
  • Collecting data you never turn into a practice priority.
✅ Success metrics
  • You chart ≥ 1 match per week and store it (Data Model).
  • Each chart yields exactly one improvement priority.
  • Your tracked weak KPI trends upward month over month (Analytics).
