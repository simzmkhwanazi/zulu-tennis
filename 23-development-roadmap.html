The 12-month plan

Set 2–3 outcome goals (ranking, results) and the process goals that drive them. Periodise the year into blocks around your key events.

BlockFocusPhysical (Athlete Dev)
Off-season (8–10 wk)Rebuild technique/weakness, add a weaponHeavy strength + aerobic base
Pre-season (4–6 wk)Sharpen patterns, point playPower, speed, court movement
In-season (events)Compete, fine-tune, scoutMaintain, taper around events
Transition (2–3 wk)Active rest, review, reset goalsRecover, mobility

⚠️ Common mistakes
  • Goals with no calendar — "improve my backhand" with no block, no metric, no review.
  • All competition, no development blocks — you plateau.
  • Never reviewing, so you repeat the same year.
✅ Success metrics
  • A written 12-month plan with blocks and 2–3 goals exists.
  • Weekly priorities trace up to a quarterly focus.
  • The annual review is completed and sets next year's top 3.
