⚠️ Common mistakes
  • Obsessing over one match's stats instead of a rolling average.
  • Chasing a "vanity" KPI (1st-serve %) by serving soft, hurting points-won.
  • Tracking numbers but never feeding the worst one into the Improvement Engine.
✅ Success metrics
  • A rolling dashboard exists and updates after each match.
  • You know your weakest KPI vs your level benchmark right now.
  • That KPI moves toward the next benchmark column over a season.
