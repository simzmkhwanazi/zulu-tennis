Match Problem Solver

A decision-support system for the situations that keep beating you. Search your problem, get the likely cause, the fix, the drill and the metric. Built on the Decision Card structure.

I keep losing to a pusher

Symptoms: long matches, you make all the errors, they barely miss. Likely cause: impatience — trying to out-hit a player who lives off your pace.

Fix: build with depth, draw the short ball, approach and finish at net; add the drop-shot/approach combo. Win with forward pressure, not bigger swings.

Drill: "approach & finish" off short balls. Metric: net points won % > 60%, UE count down. See: Pusher plan, Attacking.

I can't break a big server

Symptoms: cheap holds against you, you press on returns. Likely cause: over-swinging returns, not winning second-serve points.

Fix: stand back, block deep through the middle, attack second serves, hold your own serve easily so every game pressures theirs. Treat returns as free swings.

Drill: block-return-to-deep-target. Metric: return-in-play % & 2nd-serve-return points won. See: Return Strategy.

Lefties give me trouble

Symptoms: jammed on the ad side, your backhand gets exposed, spin surprises you. Likely cause: not adjusting for reversed spin/angles.

Fix: serve/return to their backhand, expect the ad-court kick on big points, favour deep-middle (crosscourt feeds their forehand). Practise vs lefties on purpose.

Drill: return the wide ad-serve repeatedly. Metric: win % vs lefties over a season. See: Lefty plan.

I lose tight matches / can't close out

Symptoms: lead slips when serving for it; tighten up at the end. Likely cause: changing a winning game — getting passive or over-pressing.

Fix: keep the patterns that built the lead, raise first-serve %, run your Closing-Out & Pressure-Point defaults, keep the routine identical.

Drill: serve-it-out games from 40–30 up. Metric: % games closed when serving for set/match.

I lose the long rallies

Symptoms: you crack first in extended exchanges. Likely cause: fitness, shot tolerance, or forcing too early.

Fix: raise margin/height to extend safely, improve endurance & shot tolerance, and only attack the inside ball (Home Base).

Drill: 10-ball crosscourt tolerance rallies. Metric: avg rally length you win; UE after 6+ shots. See: Athlete Development.

I lose tie-breaks

Symptoms: reach breakers but rarely win them. Likely cause: trying new things / rushing under pressure.

Fix: trusted patterns only, highest first-serve %, get the early mini-break, breathe between points. Medium aggression, low risk.

Drill: first-to-7 practice breakers daily. Metric: tie-break win %. See: Tie-break.

I'm being outpowered

Symptoms: rushed, late, pushed back. Likely cause: trading pace toe-to-toe and standing too deep.

Fix: change height & pace (slice, high heavy), move them with depth-down-the-middle, take time early or reset with defense — don't match their power.

Drill: absorb-and-reset against a feeder hitting hard. Metric: reset rate; errors while defending ↓. See: Defensive System.

I'm being outdefended (everything comes back)

Symptoms: you hit "winners" that come back, then over-press. Likely cause: attacking the wrong ball / no net finish.

Fix: attack only the genuine short/inside ball, then close the net to finish rather than going for bigger baseline winners.

Drill: short-ball "green light" + approach. Metric: approach success %, net points won.

I break down mentally

Symptoms: lose the plot after bad points/calls; double-fault clusters. Likely cause: no routine; carrying points forward.

Fix: run the 4-step Reset/Breathe/Analyze/Commit routine on every point; one external focus cue on big points.

Drill: "routine under load." Metric: win % on point-after-a-lost-point.

I play too passive

Symptoms: push the ball, wait to lose, never dictate. Likely cause: fear of errors; no clear attack trigger.

Fix: commit to attacking the inside/short ball every time it appears; pre-decide serve+1; widen the aggression dial when ahead.

Drill: "must-attack the short ball" points. Metric: winners + forced errors created ↑; short balls attacked %.

I overhit / too many unforced errors

Symptoms: spray balls, low first-serve %, errors in clusters. Likely cause: aiming the lines, attacking the wrong ball, flat low margin.

Fix: aim over a net window into zones (not lines), add margin/height, and obey ball-quality rules — construct, don't force.

Drill: 1.2 m (4 ft)-net-clearance rally game. Metric: UE rate ↓; rally balls landing deep. See: Court Geometry.

I keep missing my down-the-line changes

Symptoms: direction-change errors, especially under pressure. Likely cause: changing direction off an outside ball.

Fix: the Home Base rule — only change direction off an inside ball, in balance. Reset crosscourt off outside balls.

Drill: "cross until inside." Metric: directional-change error rate ↓.

I can't convert break points

Symptoms: earn break points, rarely take them. Likely cause: going for a winner or getting passive on the big point.

Fix: make the server play — deep return, your best pattern, force one more ball. Medium aggression, trusted shapes.

Drill: play points from break-point-up scores. Metric: break-point conversion %. See: Pressure-Point.
