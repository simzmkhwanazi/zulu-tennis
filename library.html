Core philosophy

The objective is not to hit better shots — it is to win more matches and become a better athlete on and off the court. Every recommendation in Zulu Tennis is evaluated through five lenses:

The Decision Engine (core logic layer)

Zulu Tennis runs like an in-match decision engine. The question is never "what's a nice shot?" but: given the score, situation, opponent, ball quality and my balance — what is the highest-value decision that increases my probability of winning the point? Every decision weighs 10 inputs:

⚖️ Tactical Decision Formula
  1. Score Situation
  2. Point Importance
  3. Court Position
  4. Incoming Ball Quality
  5. Player Balance
  6. Opponent Position
  7. Opponent Weaknesses
  8. Risk Level
  9. Expected Reward
  10. Recovery Position
