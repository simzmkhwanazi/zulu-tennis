2 · Top decision triggers

  • Deep & fast at me → defend high crosscourt, reset.
  • Short / inside ball → attack, take time, finish.
  • Outside ball (crossed the line) → don't change direction; reset crosscourt.
  • Opponent stretched → change direction only if I'm balanced.

3 · Opponent-type counter

  • Pusher → approach & finish; don't over-hit.
  • Counter-puncher → take time, bring them to net.
  • Power baseliner → change height/pace, deep middle.
  • Serve & volley → low returns at the feet, lob.
  • Lefty → serve/return to backhand; watch ad-court kick.
  • Big server → block deep middle; win 2nd-serve points.
  • Moonballer → take early/on the rise, then approach.
