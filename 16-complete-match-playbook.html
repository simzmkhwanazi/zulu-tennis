🎾 Drill: print the checklists and run them start-to-finish for one match.

Pre-match checklist

  • Eat & hydrate (2–3 h before); kit packed; arrive early.
  • Re-read the scouting 3-liner: neutralise / attack / exploit.
  • Choose your A-plan patterns and your identity for this match.
  • Set intention: process over outcome; run the routine every point.

Warm-up checklist

  • Dynamic movement first (never cold-serve).
  • Build through all strokes; finish with serves & returns to targets.
  • Note the conditions (wind, sun, surface) and one adjustment (environment).
  • Last 2 min: visualise your first service & return games.

First 2 games checklist

  • High first-serve %, deep returns — get in rhythm, don't force.
  • Read the opponent: weaker wing? big-point serve? movement?
  • Run your A-plan patterns; confirm or adjust the scouting read.
  • Set the aggression dial appropriately; hold/compete early.

Mid-match adjustment checklist

  • What's working / what's not? (the Set-Break planner — read it on the changeover/between sets).
  • Are you attacking the right balls? Changing direction only off inside balls?
  • Is the matchup plan right, or do you need plan B (height/pace/net)?
  • Energy & hydration check; reset the routine if drifting.

Pressure-point checklist

  • Slow down; full routine; one external focus cue.
  • Go to your Pressure-Point pattern — trusted, never new.
  • Serving: highest-% trusted location. Returning: deep, make them play.
  • Breathe out on contact; commit 100%.

Closing-out checklist

  • Keep the patterns that built the lead — same intensity.
  • Raise first-serve %; don't get safe-passive or over-press (Closing-Out).
  • One point at a time; expect their best push and answer it.

Post-match review checklist

  • Chart/aggregate stats (Match Analysis); grade video if filmed.
  • 3 things that worked / 1 thing to fix.
  • Update the opponent's scouting sheet.
  • Feed the fix into the Improvement Engine.
✅ Success metrics
  • You run every checklist for a real match.
  • First-2-games win rate ↑; fewer "no plan" losses.
  • Every match produces one improvement priority.
