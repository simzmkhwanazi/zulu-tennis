Improvement Engine

You can't fix everything at once. This engine ranks your weaknesses by how much fixing each would actually raise your win probability — so you train the right thing next.

The priority formula

Priority Score = Impact × Frequency × Ease

Impact 1–5: how much it affects match outcomes Frequency 1–5: how often it shows up Ease 1–5: how quickly it can improve

A weakness that's high-impact, frequent, and easy to fix (e.g. second-serve depth) scores huge — do it now. A rarely-relevant, hard-to-fix flaw scores low — ignore it for now, however annoying. Level B

Closing the loop

Top priority → schedule it in the Roadmap and the Practice Blueprint → re-measure in Analytics → re-score. The engine runs every few weeks; your training is never random again.

⚠️ Common mistakes
  • Training your favourite shot instead of your highest-priority weakness.
  • Trying to fix five things at once — pick one or two.
  • Obsessing over a low-frequency flaw because it's embarrassing.
✅ Success metrics
  • You always know your current #1 training priority and why.
  • The priority's KPI improves before you move to the next.
  • Practice time is spent on top-ranked items, not whims.
