Drill Library

Repeatable drills by stroke, pattern and physical quality. Every drill names a setup, reps, a constraint that forces the right behaviour, a cue, a progression, and a success metric.

Serve & return

🎾 Serve target ladder

Setup: 3 cones per box (wide/body/T). Objective: location over power. Reps: 5×12, call target before each. Constraint: must call & hit zone to score. Cue: "Spot first, pace second." Progression: add a +1 ball to the open court. Metric: location accuracy ≥ 50%.

🎾 Second-serve floor

Setup: deep target each box. Objective: 90%+ reliable kick/spin serve. Reps: 50 second serves. Constraint: make 9/10 deep or restart. Cue: "Up and out, land it deep." Progression: serve at 30–30 pressure. Metric: make rate ≥ 90% deep; DF < 2/set.

🎾 Deep-return target

Setup: server serves 1st/2nd; back-third target. Objective: neutralise deep. Reps: 6 return games. Constraint: 1st-serve returns blocked (short backswing). Cue: "Split on the toss, deep and in." Progression: attack 2nd serves. Metric: ≥ 70% in play, ≥ 40% deep.

Groundstrokes & slice

🎾 Crosscourt tolerance (10-ball)

Setup: both crosscourt only. Objective: consistency + margin. Reps: rallies of 10+; 6 rounds. Constraint: 1.2 m (4 ft) net clearance minimum. Cue: "Heavy, deep, repeat." Progression: change direction only off an inside ball. Metric: UE rate ↓; rally length ↑.

🎾 Forehand inside-out / inside-in

Setup: feeds to your backhand corner. Objective: manufacture FH weapons. Reps: 5×10 (alt IO/II). Constraint: recover fully after each. Cue: "Run around, set, drive." Progression: IO to open court, II to finish. Metric: FH winners:errors ≥ 1:1.

🎾 Backhand reset slice

Setup: partner pressures your backhand. Objective: low, deep defensive slice. Reps: 4×2 min. Constraint: slice must land past service line & stay low. Cue: "Stay down, carry it deep." Progression: slice-approach. Metric: depth ≥ 70%.

Net & overhead

🎾 Approach, split, finish

Setup: coach feeds short; you approach DTL. Objective: build then finish at net. Reps: 5×10. Constraint: split-step every time; first volley past service line. Cue: "Approach, split, deep, close." Progression: they try to pass. Metric: net pts won ≥ 65%.

🎾 Overhead under the lob

Setup: partner lobs randomly. Objective: calm, deep overhead. Reps: 4×10. Constraint: deep-middle target under pressure (angle only on short lobs). Cue: "Turn, point, reach, hit down." Progression: move back for deeper lobs. Metric: overhead error rate ↓.

Approach · passing · lob

🎾 Pass & lob mix vs net-rusher

Setup: partner approaches; you pass or lob. Objective: beat the net player. Reps: 6×2 min. Constraint: alternate dip-pass and lob; aim low at their feet or deep over backhand. Cue: "Low or over — never chest-high." Metric: pass/lob success ↑.

Patterns

🎾 Serve +1 to open court

Setup: wide serve + open-court target. Objective: first-strike combo. Reps: 5×10. Constraint: decide the +1 before serving. Cue: "Stretch, then space." Progression: live point after the +1. Metric: pts won in ≤ 3 shots ≥ 50%.

🎾 Return +1

Setup: deep return then attack the reply. Objective: turn defence to neutral/offense. Reps: 5×10. Constraint: return deep-middle, then attack only an inside ball. Cue: "Deep, then hunt." Metric: return-game points won ↑.

🎾 "Cross until inside" (Home Base)

Setup: crosscourt rally; change direction only off an inside ball. Objective: decision discipline. Reps: 6×4-min games. Constraint: illegal (outside-ball) change = lose point. Cue: "Cross, cross… now." Metric: < 2 illegal changes/game.

Defensive & pressure

🎾 Survive & reset scramble

Setup: coach hits 4–6 aggressive balls corner-to-corner. Objective: reset to neutral. Reps: 6×1-min. Constraint: 1.2 m+ (4 ft+) net clearance (height = time). Cue: "High, deep, recover." Metric: ≥ 70% reset deep.

🎾 Start at the score

Setup: games begin at a called score (0–30, deuce, ad-out). Objective: score-based decisions. Reps: rotate 6 score states. Constraint: behind = no low-% direction changes. Cue: "Read score, set dial, pick pattern." Metric: correct dial ≥ 80%.

🎾 Tie-break trust

Setup: first-to-7 breakers. Objective: execute trusted patterns under pressure. Reps: 3–5 breakers. Constraint: trusted patterns only — nothing new. Cue: "Breathe, first two points." Metric: tie-break win % ↑.

Physical (off-court)

🏋️ Acceleration starts

Setup: 5–10 m sprints from a split-step. Objective: explosive first step. Reps: 8–10, full recovery. Constraint: react to a visual cue. Cue: "Low, drive, go." Progression: multidirectional starts. Metric: 5–10 m split time ↓.

🏋️ 5-0-5 agility shuttle

Setup: 3 cones, ~4.5 m (5 yd) apart. Objective: change of direction + decel. Reps: 6–8, full recovery. Constraint: touch each line, stay low. Cue: "Brake then burst." Metric: shuttle time ↓ (see benchmarks).

🏋️ Ladder + shadow movement

Setup: agility ladder, then shadow strokes to court positions. Objective: footwork & recovery patterns. Reps: 4 ladder patterns + 4×30s shadow. Constraint: split-step + recover to bisector each rep. Cue: "Quick feet, recover home." Metric: clean footwork, balanced finishes.

🏋️ Tennis strength circuit

Setup: squat/hinge/push/pull/rotation/jump (see template). Objective: force & power base. Reps: 3–4 rounds, 5–8 reps. Constraint: quality over load. Cue: "Brace, drive, control." Progression: add med-ball power. Metric: strength & jump tests ↑.
